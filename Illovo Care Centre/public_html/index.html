Ilovu Care Centre

Ilovu Care Centre - A place where ilovu residents get fed daily

The ilovu care centre is a non-profit organisation in ilovu township that offers healthy and nutrisious meals to those who cannot affort it

We have successfully fed 10000 ilovu residents ever since our establishement in 2020

Our sponsors

Many people are coming together to sponsor this care center. To date, we have had over 50 sponsors lending us a hand. Currently our public and official sponsors include Rosebank Emporium, Gift Of The Givers, The Red Cross, Universal Bank, Ilovu Bank, Ilovu Police Department and Ilovu universty. There are also a number of private sponsors but they would like to remain annonymuous. We thank all our sponsors for helping our vision come to life!
