Ilovu Care Centre

Our Work

The Ilovu Care Center's mission is to provide food for those in desperate need. In times hard to deal you need a helping hand that will help you in life. The care center provides 2 meals per day: breakfast and super. These meals are healthy and are filling to the average person. We also offer clothes and blankets to homeless people during the winter season. We provide our meals 7 days a week 365 days a year - we never rest.


Our sponsors help us in providing food to the poor. Without them we would not be able to provide meals to families who cannot afford. Soon we will implement a delivery service to the ilovu community so that the families who need these meals will be able to get them without waiting in long qeues in the harsh weather conditions. This will also increase our efficiency and decrease the time spent handing out the food to the people
