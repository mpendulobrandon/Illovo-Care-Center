Ilovu Care Centre

About Us

The Ilovu Care Center was first established in July 12 2020. Our founding father Mpendulo started this care center because he saw that a lot of households in the community were complaining about food shortages and not affording anything to eat. He learned from his friends that this issue was very common in our community and the local municipality has not helped them ever. So, with his friends and with the help of some sponsors the Ilovu Care Center was born

We have 5 branches spread out in all parts of the ilovu township and we are looking to expand even more. With the help of our community we reach places we have never thought we would reach!
